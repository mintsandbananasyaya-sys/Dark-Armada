The code that holds the nation together

These rules are direct and meant to be followed by everyone under the banner.

Code of the Dark Armada

1
Respect all members
No harassment or personal attacks

Treat all members with respect at all times. No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.

2
Keep disagreements civil
Handle issues maturely

Keep disagreements civil and handle issues maturely.

3
Do not spam
Protect chat and voice

Do not spam, mic spam, or intentionally disrupt chat or voice channels.

4
Follow leadership in events
Events, raids, and battles

Follow instructions from leadership during events, raids, and battles.

5
No excessive toxicity
Competitive is fine, disrespect is not

Avoid excessive toxicity — competitive behaviour is fine, disrespect is not.

6
Respect the chain of command
Leadership decisions matter

Respect the chain of command and leadership decisions.

7
Obey combat orders immediately
Critical moments require speed

Orders given during combat situations must be followed immediately.

8
Raise concerns at the right time
Not during critical moments

Raise concerns respectfully after events, not during critical moments.

9
Do not leak information
Keep internal matters inside

Do not leak information, plans, coordinates, or internal discussions.

10
No spying or betrayal
No alts for other nations

No spying, alts for other nations, or betrayal of the crew.

11
Use the correct channels
Communication stays organized

Use the correct channels for their intended purpose.

12
Keep important channels clear
Listen to callouts

Keep important channels clear during events and listen to callouts.

13
Do not start wars alone
Leadership approval required

Do not start wars or conflicts without leadership approval.

14
Respect alliances and treaties
Honor agreements

Respect alliances, treaties, and protection agreements.

15
Represent the Armada professionally
Especially with other nations

Represent the Dark Armada professionally when interacting with other nations.

16
All members must contribute
Everyone strengthens the nation

All members must contribute (PvP, building, farming, scouting, etc.).

17
Rank progression is earned
By activity, effort, and teamwork

Activity, effort, and teamwork determine rank progression.

18
Cooperate with your team
Follow instructions

Follow instructions and cooperate with your team at all times.

19
Minor rule breaks
Warnings first, punishment if repeated

Minor rule breaks may result in warnings; repeated offences lead to punishment.

20
Serious offences
Immediate removal

Serious offences (leaks, betrayal, sabotage) will result in immediate removal.

21
Discipline decisions are final
Leadership has final say

Leadership decisions regarding discipline are final.

Quick clarification

☠
Act accordingly
The standard is simple

Remember: we are a disciplined and respected force — act accordingly.

❓
Can these rules change?
Yes, if leadership decides

The rules can be refined over time, but the current code is what every member is expected to follow right now.

Discipline is part of the identity.

The Dark Armada does not keep standards just for appearance. It keeps them because order, loyalty, and discipline are part of what give the nation its weight.
