Supreme command

At the top of the nation stands the highest authority. Every major action, policy, and national direction ultimately flows through this office.

👑
Tsar
Supreme leader of the Dark Armada

The Tsar is the supreme leader of the Dark Armada. All actions must go through the Tsar. This role stands above every minister, council, guard, and citizen, shaping the will and direction of the nation itself.

Final authority National direction Supreme command

Ministerial command

The ministers handle the core functions of the nation. Each one governs a critical part of Armada life, from diplomacy and building to resources and maybe even war.

🤝
Minister of Diplomacy
Head of diplomacy

Controls all communications with other nations, goes to meetings, and deals with foreign affairs. This role represents the Dark Armada abroad and shapes how others see the nation.

External relations
🏛
Minister of Architecture
Overseer of development

Oversees all developments involving builds on Armada land. If construction happens under the banner of the nation, approval and direction pass through this minister.

Build authority
⛏
Minister of Resources
Fuel of the empire

Holds authority over farmers, miners, and essential supply work. This is one of the most important jobs in the nation because resources ultimately fuel the empire.

Economic backbone
⚔
Minister of War
Brain of wartime operations

Leads the army in wartime situations and plans operations when it comes to defending the Dark Armada. This office is central to strategy, defense, and organized conflict.

Military strategy

Political and elite bodies

Beyond the ministers, the Dark Armada also relies on political structure and elite force. These groups protect the order of the nation and reinforce its authority.

🌊
Tide Council
Political party of the Dark Armada

The Tide Council serves as the political body of the Dark Armada. It represents the organized political force behind national decisions, alignment, and direction.

Political authority
🛡
The Dread Guards
Elite protectors of the hierarchy

The elite team of fighters whose main goal is to protect the hierarchy and win wars for the nation. They are the hardened edge of the Dark Armada’s internal defense and military prestige.

Elite military body
